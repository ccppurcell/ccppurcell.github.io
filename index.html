About

I am a mathematician and computer scientist. My main areas of research are structural and algorithmic graph theory. I also have interests in distributed computing and computational social choice. I recently completed a postdoc in mathematics at the University of West Bohemia under Zdeněk Ryjáček. I have also been a member of the Distributed Computing group at Aalto University under Jukka Suomela and of DIMAP at the University of Warwick under Vadim Lozin.
