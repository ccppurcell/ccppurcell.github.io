About

I am a postdoc in mathematics at the University of West Bohemia under Zdeněk Ryjáček. My research areas are: structural and algorithmic graph theory, distributed computing, computational social choice. I have been a member of the Distributed Computing group at Aalto University under Jukka Suomela and of DIMAP at the University of Warwick under Vadim Lozin.
